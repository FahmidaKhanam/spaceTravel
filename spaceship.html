OUR SPACECRAFT

At SpaceX, we operate a fleet of cutting-edge spacecraft designed to take you on unforgettable journeys through the cosmos. Each vessel in our fleet combines advanced technology, unparalleled safety features, and luxurious accommodations to ensure an out-of-this-world experience for every traveler.

Celestial Cruiser 5000

• Purpose: Perfect for orbital tourism and space station rendezvous.
• Features: Offers a smooth and comfortable ride with zero-gravity lounges, panoramic windows for breathtaking views of Earth and the cosmos, and a zero-G dining experience featuring gourmet space cuisine.
• Capacity: Can accommodate up to 50 passengers and includes amenities such as sleeping pods, recreational facilities, and immersive virtual reality entertainment.

Stellar Shuttlecraft

• Purpose: Ideal for short-distance interplanetary travel and lunar excursions.
• Features: Compact yet efficient design with rapid acceleration capabilities, zero-gravity seating, and augmented reality windows providing real-time views of celestial bodies during transit.
• Capacity: Seats up to 6 passengers and includes amenities such as refreshment stations, personal storage compartments, and on-board AI assistants for passenger comfort and convenience.

Voyager-Class Explorer

• Purpose: Designed for deep space exploration and interstellar travel.
• Features: Equipped with next-generation ion propulsion systems, advanced shielding for cosmic radiation protection, and state-of-the-art navigation sensors.
• Capacity: Accommodates a crew of up to 12 astronauts and includes spacious living quarters, research laboratories, and observation decks for panoramic views of the universe.

Nebula Yacht

• Purpose: Offers a luxurious and exclusive space travel experience for private excursions and special events.
• Features: Customizable interior design options, spacious suites with private balconies, and a dedicated concierge service to cater to the needs of discerning travelers.
• Capacity: Accommodates up to 10 guests and includes amenities such as a zero-G swimming pool, gourmet dining facilities, and a celestial observation lounge for stargazing.
